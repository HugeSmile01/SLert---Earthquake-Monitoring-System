üåä PHIVOLCS Earthquake Intensity Scale (PEIS)

The Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (PHIVOLCS) uses a 10-level intensity scale to measure earthquake effects.

I-II
Scarcely Felt
III
Weak
IV-V
Moderate-Strong
VI-VII
Very Strong
VIII-X
Devastating

Note: Intensity (I-X) measures effects felt by people and damage to structures, while Magnitude (M) measures energy released.
